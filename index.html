Our Goal

According to the Mayo Clinic, Neglected Tropical Diseases (or NTDs) are "viral, parasitic, and bacterial diseases that mainly affect the world's poorest people." More importantly however, these are diseases that are unheard of in the developed world as vaccines for NTDs have already been made. And yet, these diseases still run rampant in the third world. These diseases affect 1 in 6 people worldwide, including over half a billion people. Putting and end to these diseases is something tangible in the near future, and is important for bridging the gap between healthcare standards aross the country. With that in mind, our goal is simple: we use ad revenue generated on this very site to fund vaccines in developing countried.
